# Meeting Cost Calculator: Research Document

## Economic Impact Foundation

The document establishes that U.S. organizations hold 36-56 million daily meetings, with 187 million individual meeting experiences yearly. At an average hourly rate of $25.20, this represents "$1.4 trillion" in annual investment. Depending on ineffectiveness rates, waste ranges from $70.95 billion to $283.80 billion annually.

---

## Psychological Considerations

Research indicates a "23-minute average" recovery period after interruptions, extending to 45 minutes longer for junior staff. Social loafing increases significantly when groups exceed 8 participants. Morning meetings particularly disrupt cognitive capacity during peak focus windows.

---

## Core Calculator Features

### Input Parameters:
- Meeting duration
- Attendee count
- Role-based salary bands (aggregated for privacy)

### Warning Triggers:
- Social loafing alert when attendance exceeds 8 people
- High-visibility color change when C-suite members are added
- Cost threshold alerts suggesting asynchronous alternatives

### Output Metrics:
- Single meeting cost calculation
- Annual recurring cost projections
- "C-Suite Multiplier" for executive-inclusive meetings

---

## Integration Requirements

- Real-time cost display during calendar invite creation
- Live "dollar-burn" overlay in Zoom/Teams
- Calendar platform compatibility (Outlook/Google)

---

## Risk Management

The tool must use aggregated compensation data only, restrict dashboard access to organizers and department heads, and frame functionality as protecting focus rather than auditing expenses.

---

## Asynchronous Alternatives

The system should suggest video messaging (Loom), collaborative documents, or status channels when meetings score as low-value, and require agendas before scheduling confirmation.
